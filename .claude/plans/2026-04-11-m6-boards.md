# Milestone 6: Boards (Whiteboards)

**Date:** 2026-04-11

---

## Goal

Visual brainstorming canvases with draggable cards linked to wiki pages and connectors between them. Pan/zoom canvas. Accessible from "Boards" sidebar nav.

## Chosen Approach

Canvas-based board with HTML-rendered card nodes (for rich content/interaction) positioned absolutely. Connectors drawn via SVG overlay. Cards can be standalone (just text) or linked to a wiki page. Drag to move, click to edit.
